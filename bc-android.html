About the Project

When we started the Android redesign, we wanted to create a reading experience so engaging that our users will have a hard time putting down the app. We wanted to make it quick and easy for users to find interesting content that they'll love.

I started by conducting user research to understand how Android users interact with their phones. Based on what I learned, I redesigned the information architecture and the navigation to optimize the reading experience.

I also analyzed data on the various Related Read layout implemented on the iOS app. I used a layout that has been proven to have the highest click through rate.

New Navigation

Users can now easily discover new content and access their favorite articles with using natural swiping gestures.

Increase Engagement

I wanted to increase interactions between our users and the app for a more engaging experience. I wanted to encourage users to comment, share, and favor articles by bringing these action buttons together and place it in an easy to reach area. I also wanted to guide the interaction by creating visual hierarchy using color and button size.
