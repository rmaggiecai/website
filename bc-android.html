About the Project

When we started the Android redesign, we wanted to create a reading experience so engaging that our users will have a hard time putting down the app. We wanted to make it quick and easy for users to find interesting content that they'll love.

I started by conducting user research to understand how Android users interact with their phones. I redesigned the information architecture and the navigation to optimize the reading experience based on findings from the research.

I also analyzed data on the various Related Read layout implemented on the iOS app. I used a layout that has been proven to have the highest click through rate.

Increase Interaction

I wanted to increase interactions between our users and the app for a more engaging experience. I wanted to encourage users to comment, share, and favor articles. I brought these action buttons together and placed them in an easy to reach area. I introduced colors and variations in button sizes to help guide the interaction.
