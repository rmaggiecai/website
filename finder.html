About the Project

Extracurricular activities are an integral part of undergraduate experience. Currently, at the University of Toronto, students do not have a central medium to stay update on extracurricular events and opportunities. We set out to design a mobile app that keeps students updated on the latest extracurricular events and helps them stay involved in extracurriculars.

We used an iterative interaction design process to first understand the users, then design solution, and finally iterate on the proposed design.

Increase Interaction

I wanted to increase interactions between our users and the app for a more engaging experience. I wanted to encourage users to comment, share, and favor articles. I brought these action buttons together and placed them in an easy to reach area. I introduced colors and variations in button sizes to help guide the interaction.
