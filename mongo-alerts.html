About the Project

We wanted to help our users coordinate and communicate better with their team members. Based on user feedback, we wanted to add a new feature where users can add comments when they acknowledge an alert.

A vast majority of the traffic for MMS comes from desktops, thus I wanted to leverage the space available to organize information.

Organized Content

The table layout makes it easier for users to scan for important information.

Platform for Communication

Team members can now check who acknowledged the alert. Members can also leave comments to inform each other through the UI.
